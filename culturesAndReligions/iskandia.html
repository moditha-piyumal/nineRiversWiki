Cleanliness

Iskandia is a very clean city and it is famous for perfumed streets. Although the streets are not sprayed with perfumes of any kind, the smell of feaces and rotting food completely evades the city. The city has an underground sewage system which sends sewage and waste from the city into the sea. There are few other cities with such a waste disposal system, but, those are far too smaller than Iskandia.

Law and Order

Although Joonists are not well known for their hospitality, this city is an exception. There is a group of soldiers called law men. They do not defend the city in a war. They are good at maintining the law and order in the city and they are lenient towards outsiders. The law of Iskandia itself states that foreigners should be protected. Causing harm to a foreigner in this city can incur severe punishments. And, breaking the law is heard of very rarely in this city.

Defence Forces

This city is capable of defending itself as theyt have the largest army in the whole continent. Although if certain tribes unite they can outnumber Iskandia, the defence forces of this city and all the mercenary companies are well known for their skill and ability to destroy enemies.
