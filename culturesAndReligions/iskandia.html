Cleanliness

Iskandia is a very clean city and it is famous for perfumed streets. Although the streets are not sprayed with perfumes of any kind, the smell of feaces and rotting food completely evades the city. The city has an underground sewage system which sends sewage and waste from the city into the sea. There are few other cities with such a waste disposal system, but, those are far too smaller than Iskandia.
