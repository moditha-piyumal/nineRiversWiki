Cultural and Tribal Groups which exist in Sheethlok

The main cultural group in Sheethlok is the Joonist Culture. The Joonists dominate the Southern and Central river banks. They have established their settlements thousands of years ago. They are a warlike group of people who have advanced military technology clearly ahead of their time.

The Ukirins dominate the highest mountainous areas. They have a culture which is somewhat distinct yet also similar to that of the Joonists. They have migrated from the North of the continent and the Ukirins have an interestingly thought-provoking origin story. They are experts at defending themselves.

The Normians who reside in the Southern mountain range are great fort builders. There are many other considerably populous tribal groups including Lankans, Rohanians, Hakappans and Maisinians. Sheethlok being a large continent is home to many other minor tribes who live in secluded landscapes in the North. There are many other distinct cultures present in the continent and the scholars of the Nine Rivers have not dived deep into their cultural and religious practices.

Joonists of the River Banks

Joonists are the most prominent tribe in the Sheethlok continent and they occupy the river banks of the southern and central regions of sheethlok.

They are a tribe of warriors who follow a harsh and bloody religion called Jamapada. They earn a lot of wealth through working as mercanaries in the wars of other tribes and kindgoms. They have a culture which suits warfare and therefore are an extremely powerful military force in the known world.

Ukirin Tribes of the Central Hills

Ukirin Tribes are also a tribe prone to warfare. They follow a religion closer to the Jamapada religion of the Joonists and have certain cultural and economic ties with the Joonists.

The Ukirins wear woolen and fur clothing which suit the extreme cold climate of the mountains they live around. They are experts at fighting in the snow. They are said to have migrated from the far North. The Joonists claim that the Ukirin invaded Joonist lands and settled in the central hills. They have a unified army although the individual villages are autonomous and have elected village leaders.

Normanians in their Hill Forts

Normanians are a group of people who are afraid of outsiders. They live in forts in the Southern mountain range of Sheethlok because of their extreme fear. Due to historical wars and conflicts they hate the Joonists.

The Normanians are experts at defending their territory. The Normanian lords are not united and they fight among themselves for territorial disputes. But, they are capable of uniting against a common enemy. They are good hunters and they have snow leopards in their castles roaming freely. They treat these animals as any other man would treat a house cat and the snow leopards hunt with the Normanians.

Great City of Hakappa

The people of the city of Hakappa are known as Hakappans and they are great tradesmen. They sail the seas from Sheethlok to Nine Rivers and also to Merterra trading ivory and metal work. Their city is regarded by the Hakappans to be the oldest city in the world.

The Hakappans believe in strange gods with dozens of hands. The Hakappan monks travel the world trying to gather converts. They sometimes succeed and therefore, their city is a multi ethnic trading hub where not only goods, but also knowledge is exchanged every day and every night. They have well trained soldiers to defend their city and their trade routes. Hakappan soldiers are known by outsiders as horn heads.

The Port City of Lanka

Lanka is a port city located to the east of Sheethlok. The Lankans say that they are masters of the forest which is known in the Nine Rivers as the Dark Forest. But, the Lankans do not tell outsiders what they find in the forest.

Lankans ride horses and they have a considerably large army which they use to control the smaller tribes which have been allowed to settle around their huge city. The Lankans are clearly distinct from the other tribes which live around them but are respected by all the smaller tribes.

Maisinians who are Master Huntsmen

Misia is known as the edge of the civilized world. The people who inhabit this area are known as Maisinians. They are expert hunters and are also good fishermen. Maisinians have settled in many small towns and villages in the North East of Sheethlok.

The Maisinians are good at surviving the harsh climate of the land that they live in. They always say that monsters live beyond their lands but explorers from Nine Rivers who have accompanied Maisinian guides to the lands of those monsters have never met such beasts.

Rohanians from Rohania

The Rohanians live on the Western shore of the continent. They have a great city called Rohania. There city is the last human settlement for miles and therefore, they control a vast area of land that is rarely inhabited.

They are good farmers. They grow wheat and many other grains. The Rohanians are known to be a bit over weight by the other tribes, but, the abundance of food does help them live healthy lives. They have no horses and they have a huge army of infantrymen mostly used for defensive purposes.

The Bastion of Rostov and her subjects

The Rostovians control a vast area of land around the Bastion of Rostov. The Bastion was definitely not built by them and the people known as Rostovians have migrated to this area around 500 years ago.

The Rostovians are good fighters and they are also good pirates. They are used to plungering and pillaging villages which are not their subjects. However, they stay away from the lands of the Rohanians because of a hard lesson they learned many years ago.

Other Tribes and Cultures

Sheethlok is a large continent and the lords of the Nine Rivers do not know much about the further parts of the continent. The North is completely unknown and even the people from this continent knows little about what may be out there.

There are many tribal groups in the continent other than the ones who are trading with the lords of the Nine Rivers. They are considered less significant, but, they may even be more powerful than the known tribes and known cities. There is a great mystery surrounding the Northern part of this cold vast land.
